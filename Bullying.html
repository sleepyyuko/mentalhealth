    • Bullying

    Bullying can exist both in real life and on the internet.

    Bullying and Cyberbullying both exist throughout the world no matter whom you are. Bullying in general is a form of aggression or unwanted behaior that is repeated again and again that is targeted against people that are more vulnerable to the bully. This can harm both their social life and also both their physical and mental well-being.


    Ways of Bullying

    Physical Abuse

    Physical bullying could mean hitting, shoving, kicking, throwing something or destroying other people's belongings. Killing is also one of the most gruesome ways some may get if the situation goes worse. Due to these physical attacks, many victims also suffer from bruises or other minor injuries which is seen as pitiful to some people making it hard for these victims to continue in further life due to these past scars.

    Psychological Abuse

    If you're getting bullied, some actions like exclusion, gossiping, humiliation or even intimidation could harm the mentality of the victim. Most of the time, the victims who get affected by these actions end up killing themselves due to the trauma. Additionally, those who suffered from these acts tend to develop mental disorders for example like anxiety or depression. They also try to isolate themselves from the outside world as they have already suffered too much making it hard for them to even get contact with people that want to help the victim.

    Technological abuse

    Technological abuse or also known as cyberbullying takes effect via the internet. Most of the time, those who cyberbully tend to talk bad about someone and their personal traits. This could be to hurt their feelings or to harrass them. Some actions that correlate with technological abuse could be blackmailing, stalking or even chatting inappropriately with someone else. Although cyberbullying cannot cause many fatalities, to some with high reputations, it could shatter their reputation making it seem like the person is cruel and arrogant. To some extents, there could also be doxxing which can find the ip address of the user.

    What can we do against bullying?

    Whether you're getting physically bullied, psychologically bullied or even technologically bullied, it's important to follow these tips!

    Try to address the bully about what you are feeling. If they don't back off, pursue help from guardians.

    Spread awareness to both youths and adults about the effects of bullying making sure that there is no cause for potential bullying in the future.

    If you observe someone getting bullied, try to confront both the victim and the bully while asking about the problem.

    If you cannot reach the bully, try to ignore, block or even just laugh it off.

    If it is a wide-spread bullying, talk to the officials concerning with the proclaimed issue.

    If your reputation is at stake, try to point out evidence that favor you and not the attacker.

    Last but not least, just try to avoid potential trouble.

    Know that your life is worth more than a few vulgar words.
