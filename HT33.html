    Is someone's mental health even that important?

    Mental health is important because it affects how you think, feel, and act, and it impacts many aspects of your life, including your relationships, work, and physical health.

    Coping with stress

    Good mental health helps you deal with the stresses of life, including losses, changes, fears, and uncertainties.

    Building relationships

    Good mental health helps you form and maintain healthy relationships with others.

    Realizing your potential

    Good mental health helps you learn well, work well, and contribute to your community.

    Being physically healthy

    Good mental health can help you be physically healthy.

    Making decisions

    Good mental health is a key part of your ability to make decisions and shape the world around you.

    Feeling good

    People with good mental health may feel happy, confident, hopeful, and generally satisfied with life.

    Mental health is a basic human right and a crucial part of personal, community, and socio-economic development. Mental health can affects your brain and cognitive functioning Well, just like poor physical health can affect internal organs like your heart and gut, poor mental health can affect the way your brain works.
